{{ page.title }} is a online social network that only exists for a short period of time. In Nofacebook, any post about how good your life is such like your good looking selfie, a promotion at work, your sweet partner, is not encouraged. Instead, Nofacebook is a place for sharing the suckness about your life.

没脸圈（中文名待定）是个只会短时间存在的社交网络。在没脸圈，任何关于你多么牛x的帖子都是不被鼓励的，比如好看的自拍，比如你新举办的展览，比如即将到来的度假，比如你可爱的对象。没脸圈是一个分享不爽，失败和任何不便说出口的情绪的地方。

Nofacebook is NOT about spreading the negativeness of live. It is the complementation of the other social networks. With Nofacebook, we advocate neutralness in social sharing.

要强调的是，没脸圈并不是为了散播负能量，它也不应成为一个散发负能量的地方。我们把它看成一个我们现有的社交网络的补充。我们呼吁的是社交分享的中立性。

Nofacebook is still in development.

没脸圈在开发中。
